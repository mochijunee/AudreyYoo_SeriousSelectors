My Checkers Game

CLICK HERE TO PLAY

Choose someone to go first. When it is your turn, move any one of your checkers with the movement described below. Each player gets one turn.

Possible Movement:

Checkers move diagonally forward by one open space. If your checker makes a JUMP (over one or more adjacent enemy checkers) then you capture those checkers.

As soon as your checker reaches the opposite end of the board, it becomes a King and can move both forward and backward diagonally. If all squares adjacent to your checker are occupied then your checker cannot move.
